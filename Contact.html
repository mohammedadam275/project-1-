Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nutrition Explorer?

Nutrition Explorer is a web application designed to help users learn about healthy foods and their macronutrient content.

How can I use the Food Page?

The Food Page provides a gallery of healthy foods along with their nutritional information. Click on any food item to see detailed macros and ingredients.
